## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I skip a tour?**
A: Yes! Click "Skip Tour" or press Escape anytime.

**Q: Will my progress be saved?**
A: Yes! Your progress is automatically saved to your account.

**Q: Can I restart a completed tour?**
A: Yes! Click the help button and select "Restart" on any completed tour.

**Q: What if I navigate away during a tour?**
A: Your progress is saved. Return to the page and continue from where you left off.

**Q: Can I access tours on mobile?**
A: Yes! Tours work on all devices including tablets and phones.

**Q: Do I have to complete all tours?**
A: No! Tours are optional learning tools. Use them as needed.
